THE INSTITUTION OF ENGINEERS, IEI

Welcome to the offical page of IEI Students Chapter of National Institute Of Technology, Silchar

The Institution of Engineers (India), the IEI, is a national organization for engineers in India. It is
the world's< largest multi-disciplinary engineering professional society. It has more than
one million members in 15 engineering disciplines. The institution was established
in 1920 in Kolkata, West Bengal, and was incorporated by Royal Charter
in 1935. It is currently headquartered at 8 Gokhale Road, Kolkata.
